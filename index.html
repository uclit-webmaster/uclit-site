About UC Lit

The University College Literary and Athletic Society (UC Lit) is dedicated to fostering a vibrant, inclusive, and collegial community that enhances the student experience at University College. As elected representatives of the student body, each member of the Lit is committed to advocating for students’ interests and addressing their needs and concerns with integrity and transparency.


Our mission is grounded in the values of accessibility, equity, and transparency towards building mutual trust within the UC community. Throughout the academic year, we organize a diverse range of social, cultural, academic, and athletic events – including the UC Drag Show, Athletics Intramurals, Coffeehouse, and the Truth & Reconciliation Forum – to enrich campus life. Stay informed about upcoming programs and initiatives by following us on Instagram and other social media platforms.


UC Lit also administers graduation composite photos, manages a variety of student clubs, and provides valuable services, such as athletic gear rentals, merchandise sales, and booking facilities like the Junior Common Room for student events. Whether you are interested in starting or joining a club, participating in event planning, or connecting with our supportive community, we are here to help.


Visit us during Office Hours to access student resources, learn about new opportunities, or receive administrative support. We look forward to meeting you and supporting your journey at University College.
